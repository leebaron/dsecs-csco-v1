# DSECS — Pricing Hypothesis (Early Stage)

---

## Tier 1: Developer
**Target:** Individual developers, open-source users, researchers

- Local kernel usage (CLI demo)
- Open-source license (MIT)
- GitHub releases
- Community support

**Price:** Free (open source)

---

## Tier 2: Team
**Target:** Small teams building AI systems

- API access for integration
- Decision observability dashboard
- Structured decision logs
- Email support

**Price:** $99 / month

---

## Tier 3: Enterprise
**Target:** Regulated organizations, financial institutions, compliance-heavy teams

- Audit-grade ledger with cryptographic proof
- Compliance export (regulatory reports)
- Multi-node deployment
- Byzantine-resilient consensus
- Dedicated support + SLA
- On-premise deployment option

**Price:** Custom ($999–$10K+ / month)

---

## Pricing Principles

1. **Open source kernel** — no barrier to evaluation
2. **Value capture at enterprise tier** — compliance + audit is the revenue driver
3. **No per-decision pricing** — per-seat or per-deployment avoids chilling adoption
4. **Free tier = perpetual demo** — anyone can verify the system independently

---

## Target Segments (Early)

| Segment | Need | Entry |
|---------|------|-------|
| AI startups | Agent safety and reproducibility | Developer tier → Team tier |
| Fintech | Compliance and audit trails | Team tier → Enterprise |
| Banks/Insurance | Regulatory proof | Enterprise direct |
| AI infrastructure | Deterministic middleware | Strategic partnership |
