# DSECS — Investor Pitch

## 5 Bullets

1. **Problem** — AI systems lack decision accountability. Outputs are probabilistic, non-reproducible, and un-auditable.
2. **Solution** — DSECS introduces deterministic decision infrastructure: the first runtime that makes every AI decision constrained, scored, and replayable.
3. **CSCO** — Hard constraint gate that rejects unsafe states before execution. Zero unsafe decisions leak through.
4. **GSAI** — Bounded utility scoring (0–1 space). Every decision is quantitatively evaluated against a threshold.
5. **Ledger** — Append-only hash chain guarantees full replayability and auditability. Tampering is immediately detectable.

## Market Positioning

> "DSECS is not an AI model. It is a decision accountability layer for AI systems."

- AI agents are entering regulated environments (finance, healthcare, legal)
- No existing solution provides deterministic audit for AI decisions
- Regulatory pressure increasing (EU AI Act, SEC, FINRA)
