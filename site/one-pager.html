AI Can Decide. DSECS Decides Whether It Should Execute.

Before any AI action enters the real world, DSECS applies constraint enforcement, decision evaluation, and immutable audit — making AI decisions safe, verifiable, and enterprise-ready.

⚠️ The Problem

Today's AI systems generate actions, but enterprises lack a reliable way to answer three critical questions:

  • Is this action allowed?
  • Is this action beneficial?
  • Can we prove why it happened?

Without these answers, AI cannot safely control critical workflows.

✅ The DSECS Solution

AI Agent
→ CSCO Constraint Gate — What is allowed
→ GSAI Decision Evaluation — What should be done
→ Immutable Ledger — What can be proven
→ Approved Execution ✓
Core Capabilities

⬡ CSCO — Constraint Governance

Enforces non-negotiable business, safety, and compliance rules before execution. Every decision checked against spending limits, approval levels, and policy boundaries.

⬡ GSAI — Decision Quality

Scores decisions using multi-dimensional utility functions: safety, cost reduction, efficiency gain, compliance. Only beneficial decisions pass the threshold.

⬡ Ledger — Accountability

Every accepted or rejected decision recorded with hash-chain integrity. Full deterministic replay for audit, investigation, and compliance.

Enterprise Benefits

🛡️ Reduce Risk

Prevent invalid or unauthorized AI actions before execution. CSCO ensures every decision stays within defined boundaries.

📋 Accelerate Compliance

Deterministic, immutable records for every decision. Complete traceability for audit and investigation.

🤖 Enable Autonomous AI

Move from AI assistance to AI execution with governance. DSECS makes autonomous workflows enterprise-safe.

Technical Guarantees

🔒 Deterministic Replay

Same input always produces the same result. Full reproducibility for every decision.

🔗 Hash-Chain Integrity

🏗️ Multi-Tenant Isolation

Each tenant has independent governance policies and isolated audit trails.
